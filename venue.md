## Getting to Conference Venue

### Transportation
There are many possible routes to the conference venue, some of which are summarized as follows:

**From Kansai International Airport (KIX)**
- Route 1: 
  - Kansai Airport Station to Namba Station: Nankai Line
  - Namba Station to Osaka-umeda Station: Subway Osaka Metro (Midosuji Line)
  - Osaka-umeda Station to Ishibashi Handai-mae Station: Hankyu Railway (Takarazuka Line)
  - Walk (About 15 minutes)

- Route 2:
  - Kansai International Airport to Hotarugaike Station: [Kansai Airport Transport Limousine Bus](https://www.kate.co.jp/en/)
  - Hotarugaike Station to Ishibashi Handai-mae Station: Hankyu Railway (Takarazuka Line)
  - Walk (About 15 minutes)

**From Itami (Osaka International) Airport (ITM)**
- Route 3:
  - Osaka Airport Station to Shibahara-handai-mae Station: Osaka Monorail
  - Walk (About 15 minutes)

**From Narita International Airport (NRT) or Tokyo (Haneda) International Airport (HND)**
- Route 4: A domestic flight to ITM or KIX
- Route 5: Domestic rails (Narita express: NEX and Shinkansen: Nozomi) to Shin-osaka

**From Shin-osaka (Shin-osaka Shinkansen Station)**
- Route 6: 
  - Shin-osaka Station to Senri-chuo Station: Subway Osaka Metro (Midosuji Line)
  - Senri-chuo Station to Shibahara-handai-mae Station: Osaka Monorail
  - Walk (About 15 minutes)
