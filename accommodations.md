# {{ page.title }}
Osaka offers a wide variety of accommodation options, catering to all tastes and budgets. The conference has not contracted blocks of rooms at specific hotels, so attendees can make their own arrangements. We list some hotels in the following four areas:

- **Itami Airport** is the closest airport to [the conference venue](venue.html). It takes about 30 minutes to get to the conference venue.
- **North Osaka** is one of the areas closest to [the conference venue](venue.html). It takes about 30 minutes to get to the conference venue.
- **Umeda/Osaka** is a major commercial, business, shopping and entertainment area in Osaka. It takes about 40 minutes to get to [the conference venue](venue.html).
- **Shin-Osaka** has the bullet train (shinkansen) stop where most domestic travelers from other parts of Japan enter Osaka. It takes about 45 minutes to get to [the conference venue](venue.html).

**Please keep in mind that the listed prices are subject to change daily and/or seasonally.**
